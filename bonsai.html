An ounce of experience is more than tons of theories. And Bonsai gardening is a lively experience with nature, which is the culmination of art or hobby or obsession. It is a constant hobby due to life flora as a component and will give you a full sort of experience and happiness. There is an art, science, and bonding of generations. Bonsai translated, means tree in a pot or plant in a container, and the art as per Chinese is pending, Vamana as per Indians, but the Japanese word Bonsai become world-famous. Bonsai is the art of making miniature trees or miniature versions of what is existing in nature. The natural pattern, styles, and appearance of trees in life are converted into a small scale in an attractive container and well-displayed stand. Bonsai is not just dwarfing but also a kind of ex-situ conservation of flora/germplasm. And this hobby change the individual’s behavior, personality, English speaking skills, thought process, cognitive thinking and imagination, creativity, and aesthetic sense. In the modern world, Bonsai making is a perfect gardening technique to leave your stress behind and with full happiness. This method also rescues many unwanted plants from building sites, roadsides, old buildings, etc.… and your compassion to living things will transform through this miniature art.
